Parse cylinder line.

Function gets called if line has identifier "cy". Parse the following:

  • x,y,z coordinates of the center of the cylinder.
  • 3d normalized axis vector of cylinder. In range [-1,1] for each x,y,z axis.
  • the cylinder diameter.
  • the cylinder height.
  • R,G,B colors in range [0,255]. Also sets type and id.
    Parameters
    linePointer to current line.
    cylinderStruct where to save.
    idCurrent shape id.
