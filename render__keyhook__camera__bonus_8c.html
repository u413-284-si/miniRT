Manipulates the camera with a keypress.

Checks for keypresses and calls the corresponding function. Re-calculates different aspects of camera (viewport dimensions, base vectors, pixel grid) if necessary.

Parameters
keyKeycode of the pressed key.
camPointer to camera struct.
incIncrement value.
