Moves line one space forward and sanitizes spaces.

Move line forward if space ' ' or horizontal tab '\t' is encountered. Sets the first char to space ' '. Search for first non space or htab char. Shift line to left by copying it, getting rid of spaces. Sanitizes spaces to only one space between numbers.
